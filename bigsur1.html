Getting There

The closest airport to Big Sur is Monterey. Once you get to Monterey you can pickup a car and make the drive to the coastline. The windy roads here are some of the best portions of Highway 1 in California. Alternatively you can also fly into the SF bay area and take the scenic route down. Either way a rental car is a must for this adventure.

Day 3

A 40 minute drive, south to the quaint town of Cambria will bring you to the launch point for a guided sea kayak tour. Best Time to Travel

Summer is always a great time to visit Big Sur. It is warm and lively and there are a lot of events. You could travel in the winter and fall as well, but keep in mind that you'll need those extra layers when you're by the coast. It can get windy and cold.
