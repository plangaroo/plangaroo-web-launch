Gold Country Cave Adventure

Murphys, CA  |  2 Days

#wine #foodie #Napawannabie #cave #zipline #mountaintown

At the foothills of the Sierra Nevada you'll find the town of Murphys. This town has a population around 2000 and is often called "the next Napa". A stopping point for snow seekers in the winter, as they head to Bear Valley, this town is a hidden gem in the summertime, offering beautiful views, lakes filled with snow runoff and lots of activities that remind you that you are in Gold Country.

Getting There

The best way to get to the cute town of Murphys would be by car. You'll need to rent a car if you don't already have one. Let your Plangaroo agent know when booking. Expect about 2.5-4 hours to get there depending on traffic. You're almost at the Sierra foothill just 8 miles away from Stanislaus National Forest, an entry point for lots of wilderness backpacking and snow sports in Bear Valley.

Best Time to Travel

Summer is the best time to visit. It snows here in winter, and a lot of the wineries and the most of the activites at the cave tours operator will be closed. Of course there are other appropriate things you can do in winter such as snowshoeing cross country and winter sports in Bear Valley.
